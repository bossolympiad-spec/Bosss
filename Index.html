Questions?

Frequently Asked

Who can participate in BOSSS?
+
Any student aged 8‚Äì18 from anywhere in the world. Open to school students from Class 3 to Class 12, regardless of board or country.
How is the exam conducted?
+
All BOSSS exams are conducted fully online. Students take the exam from home or school using any device ‚Äî no travel required.
Is there a registration fee?
+
Registration is completely free for all students. Optional premium study bundles are available. The core examination will always be free.
How are medals awarded?
+
Medals are based on percentile within your age category. Top 5% = Gold, next 10% = Silver, next 20% = Bronze. All participants receive a certificate.
Can schools register as a group?
+
Yes! Schools can use our Teacher Portal to bulk-register students, track progress, and compete for the School Excellence Trophy.
What subjects are available?
+
Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Informatics (Coding), Astronomy, Geography, and Economics. Students can register for multiple subjects.

Preparation Tips

How to Ace Your Olympiad

Strategies used by gold medalists and top scorers, distilled into actionable advice.

01
Start with Fundamentals

Build a rock-solid conceptual base with NCERT before diving into advanced material. Olympiads reward deep understanding, not rote memorization.

02
Solve Past Papers

Solve 5+ years of past papers. Recognize patterns, understand the question style, and take at least one timed mock test per month.

03
Use Quality Resources

Go beyond textbooks ‚Äî Engel for Math, Irodov for Physics, Atkins for Chemistry. Free platforms like Khan Academy and PhET are excellent supplements.

04
Join a Study Group

Collaborative learning accelerates progress. Join our community forum, find study partners in your city, or participate in online olympiad discussion groups.

05
Consistent Daily Practice

2‚Äì3 hours daily of focused olympiad prep compounds dramatically. Consistency beats intensity ‚Äî small daily efforts over months create enormous results.

06
Register Early

Deadlines close months before exams. Register now, set reminders for key dates, and gather required documents well in advance.
